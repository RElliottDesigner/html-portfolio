About Me

Happy Individual with a creative soul. Artist background with many years experience in Graphic Design. Starting with a degree in Graphic Communication with a Marketing minor. Interested in web design I took a class here and there. Now I'm ready to jump into this curious and ever changing world.

Interested in many topics and varies from time to time. But my favorites are experimenting and creating, whether its playing around with photography, making handmade cards or sculpting a piece of jewelry.

Currently I spend my days working as a graphic designer making custom souvenirs for gift stores within the United States. The rest of my time is spent learning new skills, relaxing with my family, playing with our critters, and exploring.
